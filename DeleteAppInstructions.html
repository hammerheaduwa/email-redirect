Hammerhead Hub - Account Deletion Instructions

We're sorry to see you go. Please note that once your account is deleted:

  • All personal information (e.g., name, email) will be permanently erased.
  • Your previously submitted contributions will remain in the Hammerhead Hub research archive and may continue to be used for research, conservation, education, and non-commercial communication, but without attribution to you.
  • You will no longer have access to Hammerhead Hub through that account.

Instructions

  1. Open the Hammerhead Hub App and log in with the account you wish to delete.
  2. Once you are logged in, use the bottom navigation bar to access your profile page. This is the option on the farthest right of the navigation bar.
  3. From this page locate account settings in the top right corner and click on it.
  4. Under the Manage account section, select the Delete account option.
  5. Read the warning and fill type in the confirmation to permanently delete your account. You will be immediately logged out from your this account as it is being deleted.

For additional help or assistance please contact us at hammerheaduwa.help@gmail.com
